## About

> *"An archive is not just storage — it is care made permanent."*

**Sherry Archive** is a personal manga reading platform built to own your library without depending on anyone else.

It started from a simple frustration: the readers you love go down, drown in ads, or vanish overnight. This is the answer to that — a place that's yours, that stays up, that gets out of the way while you read.

---

### The Library

Browse your collection with cover art, tags, author, and status filters. Everything is organized the way you want it, not the way an algorithm decides.

### The Reader

Smooth vertical scroll, lazy-loaded pages, a UI that fades away the moment you start reading. Designed to feel like turning a page, not using software.

### Chapters & Uploads

Drop a zip file and the chapter appears — pages ordered automatically by filename, stored on S3, served fast. No manual sorting, no fuss.

### Bookmarks & Progress

Track where you left off on every manga. Resume exactly where you were, across any device.

### Recommendations

A lightweight real-time engine watches what you read and surfaces things you might enjoy next — trending titles, personalized suggestions, similar works.
