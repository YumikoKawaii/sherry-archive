**Sherry Archive** is a personal manga reading platform вАФ a quiet place on the internet to keep and read the things you love, without the noise.

It started as a weekend project to stop relying on third-party readers that go down, get flooded with ads, or disappear overnight. The idea was simple: own your library, read it your way. It grew from there.

The reader is built for comfort вАФ vertical scroll, lazy-loaded pages, a UI that gets out of the way while you read. The library side lets you browse by cover, filter by tags, track what you've finished and what you're in the middle of. Everything is private by default.

You can visit it at **[sherry-archive.com](https://sherry-archive.com)**.
